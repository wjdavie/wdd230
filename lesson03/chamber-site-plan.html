Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name:

Sanford Springvale Chamber of Commerce

This name represents the city(Sanford) and village(Springvale) in which the chamber operates.

Site Purpose:

The Sanford Springvale Chamber of Commerce works to create a positive business climate in the community through business expansion and retention. The chamber provides training, networking, business information, and resources to its members.

Scenarios:

  • What are the benefits to becoming a member of the Chamber of Commerce?
  • What other businesses are current members?
  • How much does it cost to become a member?
  • Who are the board of directors for the chamber?
  • What programs and events does the chamber offer each month?
